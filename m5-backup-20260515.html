Micro Molding

Complex Geometry. Extreme Precision.

M5's micro injection molding handles what most shops turn away — ultra-small shot sizes, complex multi-feature geometry, and tolerances measured in microns. Thermoplastics and MIM, with overmolding and insert molding in the same run.

01 / MOLDING
M5iE Micro Molding System
Purpose-built for micro-scale work — shot sizes from 0.0001 g to 0.6 g. Runs thermoplastics and MIM (metal injection molding). Complex multi-feature geometry, thin walls, and gage-quality cavities machined in-house by M5's own 5-axis.
02 / HANDLING
Robotic Part Handling
Precision part extraction directly from the mold, zero human touch. Robotic handling transfers parts to inspection or packaging without interrupting cycle time — enabling consistent, unattended production runs.
03 / INSPECTION
100% In-Line Vision Inspection
Every part inspected every cycle — not sampled. Vision system checks critical dimensions, surface defects, and flash. Bad parts are automatically rejected. SPC data captured per shot. PPAP-ready traceability built in.
04 / SECONDARY OPS
Secondary Machining
Drilling, threading, trimming, or custom secondary operations can be integrated into the same production run — eliminating separate machine moves and reducing handling. Different tooling setups accepted per program.
05 / OUTPUT
Tray Loading & Packaging
Parts go directly from inspection to organized trays with per-slot traceability. Each tray position is mapped to shot data — material lot, press parameters, and vision result. PPAP-ready documentation included.

Our Story

Built by Toolmakers

M5 Micro started as a specialty micro tooling shop — born from the vision of an owner who saw where the industry was heading. Everything was getting smaller. Work too small and too precise for most shops to touch.

Founder Jon Baklund and lead toolmaker and designer Alex Madsen launched M5 — Minnesota Micro Molding, Machining & Manufacturing. Together, they built the processes, programmed the machines, and designed everything they needed — including the patented M5 iE Micro Molding System, a proprietary approach to micro injection molding developed entirely in-house.

M5 has palletized and automated across the shop to maintain the workloads and accuracies required for the micro machined and molded parts and assemblies they've been perfecting together for years.
